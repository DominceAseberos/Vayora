# Vayora

## What is Vayora?
Vayora is a luxury travel curation and itinerary planning application. It focuses on providing users with highly personalized, premium travel experiences, bespoke destinations, and dedicated travel curators who craft unique journeys tailored to individual tastes.

## Similar Platforms
Vayora is akin to high-end travel concierge services and luxury booking platforms. If you are familiar with the following services, you'll recognize the concept of Vayora:
- **Airbnb Luxe:** Offers luxury travel with high-end properties and trip designers.
- **Inspirato:** A luxury travel subscription club with curated vacation options.
- **Black Tomato:** A bespoke luxury travel company that designs unique, immersive itineraries.
- **Exclusive Resorts:** A members-only vacation club for luxury experiences.

## Design System & UI Aesthetics
Vayora employs a premium, highly curated design system to reflect the luxury nature of the service.

### Color Theme
The color palette is earthy, elegant, and evocative of natural landscapes:
- **Ivory Sand (`#FAF6EF`)**: Primary background color, giving a soft, warm feel.
- **Deep Slate (`#1E2A38`)**: Primary foreground text color for high contrast and readability.
- **Cloud White (`#FFFFFF`)**: Used for cards and elevated surfaces.
- **Horizon Gold (`#B8943F`)**: Primary accent color for buttons, highlights, and borders.
- **Terracotta Blush (`#C6735A`)**: Secondary accent for warm highlights.
- **Driftwood (`#A9998A`)**: Muted text and subtle borders.
- **Deep Ocean (`#0E2D4A` approx)**: Used for dark contrasting sections (like footers or deep backgrounds).

### Typography
The application uses a sophisticated mix of serif and sans-serif fonts:
- **Display Font:** `Libre Baskerville` (Serif) – Used for elegant, commanding headings (H1-H6).
- **UI Font:** `Jost` (Sans-serif) – Clean and modern, used for UI elements, buttons, and navigation.
- **Body Font:** `Lora` (Serif) – Used for body copy to provide a premium, editorial reading experience.

### Animations
Micro-interactions and animations bring the interface to life:
- **Fade In Up**: A smooth entrance animation where elements gently rise and fade into view, creating an elegant reveal.
- **Ticker**: A continuous horizontal scrolling animation used for photo albums or continuous galleries.
- **Accordion**: Smooth vertical expansion and collapse for FAQ or itinerary details.

## Tech Stack & UI Libraries
- **Core:** React, TypeScript, Vite
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **UI Components:** shadcn/ui (built on Radix UI primitives)
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Animations:** Framer Motion, tailwindcss-animate
- **Carousels:** Embla Carousel
- **Toasts:** Sonner
- **Drawers:** Vaul
